Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bingo Meteor! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Game progress and scores
  • Settings preferences
  • Consent choices for advertising and analytics

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Game performance data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Crash reports and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our game services
  • Save your game progress and settings
  • Show personalized advertisements
  • Analyze game performance and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our game. AdMob may collect and use information about your device and usage patterns to show relevant ads. You can learn more about AdMob's privacy practices at Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our game. This helps us improve the game experience. You can opt out of analytics data collection in the game settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing

6.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Delete personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

7. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Advertising and Analytics Consent

When you first launch the game, you will be asked to consent to:

  • Personalized advertising
  • Analytics data collection

You can change your consent preferences at any time in the game settings. You can also opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: For advertising and analytics (you can withdraw at any time)
  • Legitimate Interest: For game functionality and improvement
  • Contract Performance: To provide the game service

12. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Game progress data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
